About the Platform

NUTECH Vault

A digital memory archive built by students, for the entire NUTECH community — designed to preserve university life for generations to come.

Our Mission

Why We Built NUTECH Vault

University life passes faster than anyone expects. The friendships, the late-night study sessions, the first lecture, the final exam, the graduation ceremony — these moments deserve more than a fading phone gallery.

NUTECH Vault gives every member of the NUTECH family a permanent, institutional home for their memories — free, secure, and always accessible.

Built on Supabase

This platform uses Supabase for real-time database, secure authentication, and cloud storage — ensuring your memories are always safe.

How it works
Sign up → Upload memories → They save to Supabase cloud → Share with the NUTECH community forever.

🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Introduction

NUTECH Vault collects your name, student ID, department, batch year, and email when you register. Any content you upload — memories, photos, or comments — is stored securely on Supabase cloud infrastructure.

2. Privacy Controls

You control who sees your memories: 🌍 Public (all verified members), 🏛️ Department Only (only your department), or 🔐 Private (only you). You can change visibility at any time.

3. Data Security

We use encrypted HTTPS connections, row-level security policies, and Supabase's industry-standard authentication. We never sell your data to third parties.

4. Contact

For questions or account deletion requests, email vault@nutech.edu.pk. We respond within 2–3 business days.

📜 Terms of Use

Last updated: January 2024

1. Eligibility

NUTECH Vault is exclusively for current students and alumni of the National University of Technology (NUTECH), Islamabad. By registering, you confirm this eligibility and that all information provided is accurate.

2. Acceptable Use

Do not upload defamatory, abusive, or hateful content. Do not share content that violates another person's privacy or infringes intellectual property rights. Do not impersonate other students or faculty. Violations may result in account suspension.

3. Content Ownership

You retain ownership of content you upload. By sharing, you grant NUTECH Vault a non-exclusive licence to display your content per your chosen privacy settings.

4. Contact

For questions regarding these Terms, contact vault@nutech.edu.pk.
